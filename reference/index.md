# Package index

## Datasets

Included datasets.

- [`acs_poverty`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/acs_poverty.md)
  : US County Poverty Rates by Age Group

  : US County Components of Population Change
- [`asasec`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/asasec.md) :
  American Sociological Association Section Membership
- [`bad_date`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/bad_date.md) :
  Dates in character form
- [`color_table`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/color_table.md)
  : A table of hex color values related to types of color blindness
- [`counties_sf`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/counties_sf.md)
  : US County geometries and demographic data
- [`county_data`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/county_data.md)
  : Census Data on US Counties
- [`county_map`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/county_map.md)
  : US County map file
- [`edu`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/edu.md) : Years of
  school completed by people 25 years and over in the US.
- [`election`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/election.md) :
  US Presidential Election 2016, State-level results
- [`election24`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/election24.md)
  : US Presidential Election 2024, State-level results
- [`election24_county_df`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/election24_county_df.md)
  : US County-level Presidential Election data, 2024
- [`elections_historic`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/elections_historic.md)
  : US Presidential Election vote shares
- [`farsinvolved`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/farsinvolved.md)
  : Child Pedestrians involved in Fatal Motor Vehicle Crashes, 2009-2023
- [`fredts`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/fredts.md) :
  Monetary Base and S&P 500 series
- [`gss_sm`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/gss_sm.md) :
  General Social Survey data, 2016
- [`gss_lon`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/gss_lon.md) :
  General Social Survey data, 1972-2024
- [`lawschools`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/lawschools.md)
  : US Law School Enrollments 1963-2015
- [`maunaloa`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/maunaloa.md) :
  Mauna Loa Atmospheric CO2 Concentration
- [`oecd_le`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/oecd_le.md) :
  Life Expectancy in the OECD, 1960-2023.
- [`oecd_sum`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/oecd_sum.md) :
  Life Expectancy in the OECD, 1960-2023
- [`okboomer`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/okboomer.md) :
  Monthly Births in the U.S., 1933-2015
- [`opiates`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/opiates.md) :
  Opiate-Related Deaths in the United States, 1999-2020
- [`organdata`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/organdata.md)
  : Organ donation in the OECD
- [`preg`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/preg.md) : An
  untidy table of data
- [`preg2`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/preg2.md) : A
  wider table of untidy data
- [`states_sf`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/states_sf.md)
  : US State geometries
- [`studebt`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/studebt.md) :
  Student debt data
- [`titanic`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/titanic.md) : A
  table of survival rates from the Titanic
- [`yahoo`](https://kjhealy.github.io/socviz/reference/yahoo.md) : Yahoo
  Revenue and Employees
